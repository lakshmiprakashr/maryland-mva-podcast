**Q17. [SYNTHESIS]** What are the key differences between crash scenarios requiring different responses? Compare injury crashes, no-injury with immobilized vehicles, and no-injury with mobile vehicles.

- A) Only required for commercial vehicles
- B) Injury crash: remain at scene, call 911, report details, do not move vehicles. No injury but vehicle can't move: call 911 for police assistance, stay away from traffic.
