# Chapter 22 — Rookie Manual — Answer Key

### Question 1
**Correct Answer:** D) A minimum of 60 hours, including at least 10 hours of nighttime driving.

**Explanation:** Maryland requires 60 hours of supervised practice for drivers under 25. Of these, at least 10 must be at night. The hours are in addition to the 6 hours from driver education.

**MVA Test Trap:** Don't forget the nighttime component — it is part of the 60 hours, not additional.

**Common Mistake:** Confusing the under-25 requirement (60 hours) with the 25+ requirement (14 hours).
