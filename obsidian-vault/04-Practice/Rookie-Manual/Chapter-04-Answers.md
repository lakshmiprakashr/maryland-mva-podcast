# Chapter 04 — Rookie Manual — Answer Key

## Answers with Explanations

### Question 1
**Correct Answer:** A) Yes. You may use your backup camera and the vehicle's onboard radar system during the skills test.

**Explanation:** Maryland allows the use of backup cameras and onboard radar systems during the skills test, as these are considered standard safety features.

**MVA Test Trap:** Don't confuse backup camera with parking assist. Camera is allowed; parking assist is not.

**Common Mistake:** Assuming all technology is prohibited during the skills test.

**Reference:** See chapter

### Question 2
**Correct Answer:** A) No. You may not use the parking assist feature during the skills test.

**Explanation:** While backup cameras and onboard radar are allowed, parking assist is specifically prohibited because it takes over steering control, which the MVA wants to test you on.

**MVA Test Trap:** Don't confuse parking assist with backup camera. They are different technologies with different rules.

**Common Mistake:** Assuming all driving assistance technology is treated the same way.

**Reference:** See chapter

### Question 3

### Question 18

**MVA Test Trap:** Don't confuse PBJ with a regular conviction. Both trigger the same 9-month extension.
