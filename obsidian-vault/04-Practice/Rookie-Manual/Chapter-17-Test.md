**Q18. [SYNTHESIS]** Explain the relationship between the three steps of parallel parking: setting up, backing in, and adjusting. Why must they be done in this order?

- A) Not specified in the manual
- B) Only required for commercial vehicles
- C) Varies by county
- D) Setting up (parallel to the front vehicle, 2-3 feet apart) establishes your starting position. Backing in with sharp steering gets you into the space at an angle. Adjusting (shifting to drive, steering opposite) straightens you out and positions you within 12 inches of the curb. Each step creates the conditions for the next — skipping or reordering them makes the maneuver fail.

The GDL system is designed to build skills progressively through supervised practice. Mastering parking under supervision before driving alone reduces the risk of minor collisions that escalate into dangerous situations.
