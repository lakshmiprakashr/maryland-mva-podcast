# Chapter 08 — Master Memorization Sheet

## Chapter Summary

Lesson 3 covers **position within the lane** (three positions), **common backing errors**, and the **coach's checklist** for readiness. Also introduces **entering traffic from the side of the road**, **approaching intersections**, and **school bus stopping rules**. This chapter bridges parking lot practice to real-world road driving.

## Why It Matters

Lane positioning directly affects your safety margin from parked cars, oncoming traffic, and pedestrians. Entering traffic and intersections are the highest-risk driving situations for new drivers. The school bus law is one of the most commonly tested and most strictly enforced Maryland traffic laws.

## High-Yield Facts

- **Three lane positions:** Center (1) = default/most common; Left (2) = hills, curves, next to parked cars; Right (3) = preparing for right turn
- **All positions must keep the vehicle entirely within the lane lines**
- **Entering traffic steps:** Check rear/sides/front, signal, select safe gap, drive into appropriate lane, turn off signal, accelerate to speed
- **Intersection steps:** Check for vehicles/pedestrians/signs/devices, adjust speed, tap brake to alert drivers behind, full stop at stop signs and red lights, ensure intersection is clear before proceeding on green
- **School bus rule:** If NO roadway divider between you and the bus AND the bus has flashing red lights and stop sign extended, you MUST STOP AND WAIT until lights off and sign down
- **Coach's checklist:** Controls known? Pre-entry check done? Brakes smoothly? Backs up correctly? ALL must be "Yes"

## MVA Test Traps

| Trap | Correct Answer |
|------|---------------|
| "You can pass a school bus with red lights on if there's a median" | Only a physical roadway divider (not a painted median) exempts you; you must still stop |
| "Center position is only for highways" | Center is the default position for ALL driving situations |
| "A green light means go immediately" | You must ensure the intersection is clear before proceeding, even on green |
| "Left position is for passing" | Left position is for hills, curves, and alongside parked cars |
| "Tap the brake only when stopping" | Tap the brake when approaching an intersection to alert drivers behind you, even if not stopping |

## Common Mistakes

- Not checking all directions (rear, sides, front) when entering traffic
- Forgetting to turn off the turn signal after entering traffic
- Proceeding through an intersection on green without checking if it's clear
- Not coming to a FULL and COMPLETE stop at stop signs and red lights
- Stopping too far from the stop sign or too far into the intersection
- Misunderstanding the school bus stopping rule

## Memory Tricks

- **"1-2-3 Lane"** — Position 1 = center (default), Position 2 = left (conditions), Position 3 = right (right turn prep)
- **"Check, Signal, Gap, Go, Cancel, Speed"** — Entering traffic sequence
- **"Look, Slow, Tap, Stop, Check, Go"** — Intersection approach sequence
- **"Red bus, no divider = STOP"** — School bus rule: flashing red + stop sign + no divider = stop and wait
- **"Green means check, not go"** — Always verify the intersection is clear before proceeding on green

## Must-Know Signs and Signals

- **School bus stop arm** — Extended stop sign on the side of the bus with flashing red lights
- **Stop sign** — Full and complete stop required; one of the most tested items on the MVA exam
- **Traffic signals** — Green = proceed if clear; Yellow = stop if safe; Red = stop completely
- **Turn signals** — Required before entering traffic and before turns

## Maryland-Specific Laws

- **School bus stopping law (MD Transportation Article 21-706):** Drivers must stop when approaching a school bus from either direction on a highway without a physical median divider when the bus displays flashing red lights and the stop arm is extended. Violation carries heavy fines and points.
- **Full stop requirement:** Maryland law requires a full and complete stop at all stop signs and red lights — not a rolling stop
- **Signal requirement:** Must signal before entering traffic from a parked position

## One-Minute Review Checklist

- [ ] Lane positions: Center (1, default), Left (2, hills/curves/parked cars), Right (3, right turn prep)
- [ ] Entering traffic: Check rear/sides/front, signal, safe gap, enter lane, cancel signal, accelerate
- [ ] Intersections: Check, adjust speed, tap brake, full stop at red/stop signs, clear intersection on green
- [ ] School bus: Red lights + stop arm + no roadway divider = STOP and wait
- [ ] Green light: Does NOT mean go immediately — check intersection is clear first
- [ ] Stop completely: Full stop at all stop signs and red lights — no rolling stops
- [ ] Coach's checklist: All four items must be "Yes" before progressing
